About Us

Kenya's Premier B2B Distribution Network

Kenya FMCG Wholesalers and Distributors Network Ltd is a strategic matchmaking platform connecting manufacturers with vetted, financially stable distributors across Kenya. Since 2017, we've been building the infrastructure needed to optimize the distribution of fast-moving consumer goods throughout the nation.

Our Mission

To create the most efficient and reliable distribution network in East Africa, enabling seamless market access for quality products.

Our Vision

To be the backbone of consumer goods distribution in Kenya, known for excellence, integrity and innovation.

Our Core Values

Trust

Building reliable partnerships through transparency and integrity

Capacity

Delivering scale and reach across the entire Kenyan market

Agility

Adapting quickly to market changes and client needs

Growth

Continuously expanding capabilities and opportunities
